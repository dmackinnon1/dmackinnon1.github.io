Take All

Take All puzzles have these simple rules:
  1. The pieces are all the same colour, and must capture each other.
  2. The King make the last capture and be the last piece standing.
  3. Every move must be one that takes another piece.
  4. Each piece can only make up to two moves.
This puzzle was inspired by the NYT "Capture" puzzle.
