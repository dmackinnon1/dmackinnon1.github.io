Joust

You are moving a chess piece around a board. Your adversary is also moving around the board, using the same move options as the type of chess piece that you have selected.

Both you and your adversary are not allowed to land on a square that has already been visited by either of you.

Your goal is to block your adversary from making any further moves before they block you.

This game was inspired by "Radioactive Pooping Knights."
